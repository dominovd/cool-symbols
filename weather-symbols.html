How to use weather symbols

Click any symbol to copy. The text-style sun ☀ and cloud ☁ pair well with minimal aesthetic bios and weather-app interfaces. Use ❄ as a snowflake bullet in winter-themed lists or ☂ next to umbrella shop links. Emoji versions like 🌧 ⛈ 🌪 are heavier and better for individual emphasis — "rainy day vibes 🌧" — rather than repeated decoration. The crescent moon ☾ and full moon emoji series (🌕 🌖 🌗 🌘) double as time-of-day markers and astrology/aesthetic accents. Combine snowflake ❄ with stars ✦ for winter wonderland decorations in cards and posts.

Where weather symbols shine

Weather forecast posts

Quickly indicate weather in event posts without writing it out — "☀ outdoor market this Saturday" or "⛈ event moved indoors." Self-explanatory across languages.

Travel and vacation captions

Mark the vibe of a destination in one character — ☀ for beach, ❄ for ski, 🌧 for atmospheric city — in Instagram captions and TikTok video descriptions.

Mood and journal posts

Weather symbols as emotional metaphors are an old internet tradition — ⛈ for a bad day, ☀ for hopeful, 🌫 for unclear. More expressive than a single sad/happy emoji.

Seasonal store and product copy

Mark seasonal collections with ❄ winter, 🌸 spring, ☀ summer, 🍂 autumn (the autumn leaf is in the food/nature emoji set, see Flowers page). Quick visual sort.

Moon-phase astrology and aesthetic

Use the moon-phase sequence (🌑 🌒 🌓 🌔 🌕 🌖 🌗 🌘) as decorative dividers between sections of an astrology post or aesthetic bio.

Frequently asked questions

What's the difference between ☀ and ☀️?
☀ alone (Black Sun With Rays, U+2600) is the plain text-style sun glyph, displayed in the surrounding font color. Adding the variant selector U+FE0F (which you don't usually see, but renders the character as ☀️) makes the system render it as a full-color emoji. Most chat apps now render ☀ as ☀️ automatically when it stands by itself, so the distinction matters less in everyday use than it once did.
Is there a wind symbol?
There are two close options: 🌬 (Wind Face) shows a face blowing wind, useful in weather captions; 🌪 (Tornado) is a strong-wind/storm marker. For directional wind, combine with arrow symbols — 🌬→ for east wind. No pure 'wind' arrow exists in Unicode itself.
Do moon phases work as decorative dividers?
Yes, the eight moon-phase emoji (🌑 🌒 🌓 🌔 🌕 🌖 🌗 🌘) make great section breaks in aesthetic profiles, especially astrology and witch-aesthetic content. They're visually consistent in size and color across iOS, Android, and most chat apps. Use the full eight-phase sequence for a dramatic horizontal divider.
