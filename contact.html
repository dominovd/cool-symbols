Get in touch

We read every email. Whether it's a bug report, a feature idea, a partnership pitch, or just a hello — write to us.

Email
info@cool-symbols.net

Typical reply within 2–3 business days.

What to write about

  • Bug reports. If something is broken — wrong copy-paste result, AI returning errors, layout glitches on a specific device — tell us what you did, what you expected, and what happened. Screenshots help.
  • Feature requests. Want a specific font style added? A new AI mode? A symbol category we're missing? We're a small team but we read everything.
  • Partnerships. If you're building something that could plug into the AI tools (a bot, an extension, a content site), let's talk.
  • Press and licensing. If you'd like to feature the tool somewhere or have licensing questions about the (mostly Unicode-based) content.

What we can't help with

We can't recover lost generations — nothing is stored on our side. We can't unlock more daily AI generations on request; if you need higher limits, watch for a future Pro tier announcement. And we can't write content for you on demand — the AI tools are self-serve.
